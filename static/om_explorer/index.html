Each of the three studies varies a different parameter controlling the simulated fishery. The commercial catchability variance study looks at the magnitude of variation of the catchability variation in the commercial fleet. The spatial catchability shared with survey study fixes the variation in catchability for the commercial fleet, but varies what proportion of that variation is experienced by the survey fleet. The intensity of commercial fleet preference varies the intensity of preferential selection by the commercial fleet.
